Describe France

France, officially the French Republic, is a leading Western European nation, renowned for its rich culture, history, and influence in art, cuisine, and fashion. As the EU's largest country by area, it boasts diverse landscapes—plains, coasts, and Alps—and attracts over 90 million annual tourists. Its capital is Paris

Recipe for cake

A classic vanilla cake is made by creaming 3/4 cup softened butter with 1 3/4 cups sugar, adding 2 eggs and 1 tbsp vanilla, then alternating 2 1/2 cups flour/2 1/2 tsp baking powder with 1 1/4 cups milk. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes. Room temperature ingredients ensure the best texture.
